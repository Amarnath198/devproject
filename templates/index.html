How to Use Our Event Management System

Log In: Access the system by logging in with your credentials.

Dashboard: After logging in, you'll be taken to your dashboard. Here, you can see an overview of your events, upcoming schedules, and notifications.

Create an Event: To create an event, click on the "Create Event" button. Fill in the required details, such as the event type, name, dates, and description.

Review and Confirm: Before submitting, review the event details to ensure everything is accurate. Once confirmed, your event will be added to the system.

Track and Manage: Use the dashboard to manage your events. Track participant enrollments, send notifications, and update event details as needed.
